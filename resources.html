Core AI Tools (University Recommended)

These are the primary, university-approved tools for your academic work. They are free to use with your university login and offer enhanced data protection.

Microsoft Copilot (General)

A general-purpose AI assistant for text generation, brainstorming, and editing.

  • Best for: Quick questions, image generation, and general writing help.
  • Permitted at: All levels (depending on the task).

NotebookLM (from Google)

A source-grounded AI that only works with documents you upload. It is safer because it cannot "hallucinate" facts or cite sources that do not exist. However, it can 'hallucinate' from the uploaded document, so check the links it provides in the document do actually fully support the claims made.

  • Best for: Summarising academic sources, literature analysis, and research organisation.
  • Permitted at: Levels 3-6 for academic work.

Custom Copilot Agents

These are specialized AI agents, created by your tutors, that must be added to the main Copilot interface for best use.

Academic Rigour Assistant

Provides evidence-based, carefully considered responses for academic work.

  • Best for: Understanding research evidence, checking accuracy, and exploring limitations.
  • Permitted at: Levels 3-6.

Socratic Tutor

Helps you learn by asking you questions, rather than giving direct answers.

  • Best for: Understanding difficult concepts and developing critical thinking.
  • Permitted at: All levels (as a learning tool).

Research & Verification Databases

AI can generate content, but only these sources can verify it. You must use these to find high-quality academic sources.

SPORTDiscus

A specialist database for sport science and sport medicine research.

  • Best for: Finding peer-reviewed sport science research.
  • Access: Through the university library website.

Google Scholar

A broad search engine for academic research. Good for quick searches and checking citations.

  • Best for: Quick searches, finding specific articles, and checking citations.
  • Access: Free, no login required.
