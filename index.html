AI Skills Development Programme

Sport Science Edition | Birmingham Newman University

Welcome to AI Skills Development

This programme helps you develop the AI literacy skills you need for your sport science studies. We use a clear 6-level framework to ensure you understand when and how to use AI appropriately in your assessments.

Important: Every assessment specifies which AI level is permitted. Using AI inappropriately is academic misconduct.

Choose Your Pathway

Select the pathway that matches your current needs and skill level. You can complete multiple pathways as your skills develop.

Foundation Pathway

For: New to AI or need Level 1-3 skills

Covers:

  • Understanding the 6-level framework
  • Using AI for brainstorming (Level 2)
  • AI-assisted editing (Level 3)
  • NotebookLM for research
  • Basic fact-checking
  • Finding sport science sources

Time: 60-90 minutes

Perfect for: Level 1 students, SPU412 assessment

Start Foundation Pathway
Development Pathway

For: Need Level 3-4 skills for essays and reports

Covers:

  • Advanced AI editing techniques
  • Critical evaluation of AI outputs
  • Advanced NotebookLM strategies
  • Using Academic Rigour Assistant
  • Comprehensive documentation
  • Verification protocols

Time: 90-120 minutes

Perfect for: Level 2 students working on literature reviews

Start Development Pathway
Advanced Pathway

For: Need Level 4-5 skills with advanced critical evaluation

Covers:

  • Advanced critical evaluation techniques
  • Sophisticated research workflows
  • Beginning collaborative AI (Level 5)
  • Quality assurance at scale
  • Discipline-specific applications
  • Ethical analysis of AI use

Time: Self-paced with challenges

Perfect for: Level 3-4 students, advanced projects

Start Advanced Pathway
Expert Pathway

For: Need Level 5-6 skills for dissertation or major projects

Covers:

  • AI as research collaborator
  • Iterative refinement techniques
  • Comprehensive documentation
  • Quality assurance protocols
  • Dissertation applications
  • Advanced critical evaluation

Time: Ongoing for extended projects

Perfect for: Final year students, dissertation work

Start Expert Pathway

Not sure which pathway to choose?

Start with Foundation if:

  • You are new to using AI in academic work
  • Your current assessments only permit Levels 1-3
  • You are in Level 1 (first year) of your programme

Move to Development when:

  • You are comfortable with Foundation skills
  • Your assessments permit Level 4 (e.g., literature reviews)
  • You need to critically evaluate AI-generated content

Progress to Advanced when:

  • You are confident with Levels 1-4
  • You need sophisticated critical evaluation skills
  • You are preparing for dissertation-level work
  • You want to explore ethical and professional implications

Move to Expert when:

  • You are working on your dissertation or final year project
  • Your assessments require Level 5-6 collaboration
  • You need comprehensive documentation strategies

Quick Start Guide

If you need help right now with an assignment:

  1. Check your assignment brief - Which AI level is permitted?
  2. Choose your pathway:
    • Level 1-3? → Foundation Pathway
    • Level 3-4? → Development Pathway
    • Level 4-5 advanced? → Advanced Pathway
    • Level 5-6 dissertation? → Expert Pathway
  3. Follow the pathway modules
  4. Complete your AI Evidence Form
  5. Still stuck? Check the FAQ or contact your lecturer
