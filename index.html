Welcome to AI Skills Development

This programme helps you develop the AI literacy skills you need for your sport science studies. We use a clear 6-level framework to ensure you understand when and how to use AI appropriately in your assessments.

Important: Every assessment specifies which AI level is permitted. Using AI inappropriately is academic misconduct.

Choose Your Pathway

Select the pathway that matches your current needs and skill level. You can complete multiple pathways as your skills develop.
