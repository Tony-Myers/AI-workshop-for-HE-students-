Start Here

This site supports your development as a confident and responsible user of AI at Birmingham Newman University. It is built around the university’s 6-level framework for permitted AI use in assessments. Your assignment brief will tell you which level applies – the pages here will help you work within that level while still getting the most from AI.

Choose Your Pathway

The pathways are designed to support you at different stages of your degree. You can move between them as your confidence and the permitted AI level in your modules increase.

Foundation Pathway

For students who are new to AI or working mainly at Levels 1–3. Learn the basics of permitted AI use, idea generation, and AI-assisted editing, with a strong focus on academic integrity.

  • Foundation Pathway page
  • AI basics refresher

Development Pathway

For students who are ready to use AI more actively at Levels 3–4. Build skills in AI editing, critical evaluation of AI output, and documenting your AI use for coursework.

  • Development Pathway page
  • Prompting techniques

Advanced Pathway

For confident users working with Level 4–5 tasks. Engage in challenge-based activities exploring bias, reliability, and advanced research workflows with tools like NotebookLM and specialist agents.

  • Advanced Pathway page

Expert Pathway

For final-year projects and dissertations at Levels 5–6. Focus on AI as a collaborator, rigorous documentation, and evaluating AI’s impact on your research and writing.

  • Expert Pathway page
  • Specialist tools & agents
